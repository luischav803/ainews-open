## 📌 About this project

The ainews-open project gathers news about artificial intelligence and summarizes the information into a daily report. It uses technology to monitor sources, process the text, and send the result to your preferred platforms. The system writes these summaries in Chinese to keep you informed about global developments in technology.

## ⚙️ System requirements

Ensure your computer meets these standards before you begin:

*   Operating System: Windows 10 or Windows 11.
*   Memory: At least 4 gigabytes of RAM.
*   Storage: At least 500 megabytes of free disk space.
*   Internet Connection: A stable connection to fetch news data.

## 📄 Configuring your news feed

The application organizes data based on sources you define. 

1. Within the control panel, click the Settings tab.
2. Add your preferred RSS feeds in the list provided.
3. Select the AI topics you want to track.
4. Save your changes to allow the application to begin gathering data.

## 📱 Setting up delivery channels

You can have the daily digest sent to different locations.

1. Navigate to the Channels tab.
2. Choose from Feishu, Telegram, or WeChat.
3. Follow the specific link provided for your chosen channel to obtain an access key.
4. Paste the access key into the application settings.
5. Click the test connection button to verify the link works.

## 📅 Daily digest schedule

The system runs on an automatic timer to create reports. You can change when these reports appear.

1. Go to the Schedule menu.
2. Choose the time of day you wish to receive your digest.
3. Select the days of the week for report generation.
4. Click Apply to solidify your schedule.

## 🔍 Frequently asked questions

**Why does the system show a terminal window?**
The window performs the work of gathering data from the internet. This is how the program monitors the news.

**What happens if the internet goes down?**
The program pauses its activity. Once your connection restores, the software resumes gathering news automatically.

**Does this software store my private information?**
The software stores settings locally on your computer. Your account keys for telegram or other channels remain on your device.

**Can I change the language of the reports?**
The system creates reports in Chinese by default. You can adjust the language output in the Advanced Settings menu if you prefer other options.

## ⚠️ Troubleshooting common issues

If you encounter difficulties, check these common fixes:

*   Application does not open: Ensure no other instance of the program is currently running. Check your task manager to close frozen processes.
*   No news appears: Verify your internet connection. Check that your RSS feed list contains valid, active links.
*   Messages do not send: Verify that your access keys for Telegram or Feishu are correct. Ensure you have network permissions enabled for this application in your firewall settings.
*   Application is slow: Close other memory-intensive programs like web browsers with many open tabs to free up resources.
